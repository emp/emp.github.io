This online part catalog for 30,000+ vehicles lets users find products using a multi faceted search interface with a heavy mobile focus giving the app a native feel even in mid-range Smartphone browsers.

Thanks to the UX/Performance accomplished, the competitive edge against existing catalogs was immediately obvious among stakeholders, making the frontend architecture choices first class in the project's success.
